WhiteWhale

A hunted-leviathan action-adventure inspired by Moby Dick and Subnautica.

You ARE the Legend

You are the legendary White Whale — powerful, intelligent, navigating a vast ocean while pursued by an obsessed hunter. Survive, grow stronger, and decide your fate: evade forever, confront Ahab, or embrace your legendary destiny.

▶ Play Demo Star on GitHub 🚢 Coming to Steam

🎯 Hunted Protagonist

Flip the predator-prey dynamic. You are the hunted — Ahab is the hunter. Every encounter escalates as his obsession grows.

🌊 Ocean Exploration

Navigate 4 interconnected zones — coral flats, deep trench, ice shelf, and open sea. Each teeming with life and danger.

🐋 Whale Abilities

Hunt, navigate via echolocation, and communicate through song. Your cetacean senses are your greatest weapon.

⚔️ Dynamic Nemesis

Ahab's obsession escalates over time. Encounters grow more intense. Can you outwit a hunter driven by legend?
