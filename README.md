# WhiteWhale — Preview

A hunted-leviathan action-adventure inspired by Moby Dick and Subnautica.

## What is WhiteWhale?

You ARE the legendary White Whale — a powerful, intelligent cetacean navigating a vast ocean while pursued by an obsessed hunter (Ahab). Survive, grow stronger, and decide your fate: evade forever, confront your hunter, or accept your legendary destiny.

**Duration:** 8–10 hours  
**Genre:** Action-adventure, exploration, myth-meets-ecology  
**Inspired by:** Moby Dick, Subnautica, real cetacean behavior

## Core Experience

- **Hunted Protagonist:** Flip the predator-prey dynamic — you're the hunted whale, Ahab is the hunter
- **Ocean Exploration:** Navigate 4 interconnected zones (coral flats, deep trench, ice shelf, open sea)
- **Whale Abilities:** Hunt, navigate via echolocation, communicate through song
- **Dynamic Nemesis:** Ahab's obsession escalates — encounters grow more intense over time
- **Player Choice:** Evade, confront, or embrace your legendary fate
